About this page

Yes, I said it. I don’t like About Me sections but here goes. My first real job was at Six Flags Over Georgia. After a few weeks I distinguished myself as a hard worker. The Red Tags and Blue Tags noticed and eventually promoted me to Red Tag at the Cotton Candy stand. I eventually was promoted to a Lead Red Tag position as manager of the Dixie Dog Hot Dog stand. As I think back to that experience, I don't know if I realized it but that stand was really the first restaurant visitors say after entering the park.


McDonalds

The Golden Arches was and still is a starting place for many young people. I quit Six Flags to be a swing manager at McDonalds. I started at the Northside Drive McDonalds store in Atlanta. Next I was transfered to the Lenox Square store, one of the elite McDonalds in Atlanta. I learned a lot..


C&S (Citizens and Southern National Bank)

I tell everyone I was to preverbal mailroom to manager stories. I was eventually promoted to AVP at Bank of America. My position was manager, Treasury Management Implementation Specialist.

What I Do

I work at a financial institution. My job is manager of electronic services. I work with a team that supports our online banking web application. We help with logon problems, navigating the website, resolving issues with bill pay and person to person payments. I really like my job. I have been in the FI industry for more than 30 years. So I know banking and tech.
